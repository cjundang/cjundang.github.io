About Me

I received the joint B.Sc. and M.Sc. degree in computer science and Ph.D. degree in computer engineering from the Price of Songkla University, Thailand, in 2005 and 2011, respectively. He is currently a Researcher and a Lecturer with the School of Informatics, Walailak University, Thailand. My research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence in the domains of the Internet of Things (IoT), cybersecurity, intelligent network monitoring systems, and data science, with a particular emphasis on enhancing cybersecurity and network monitoring capabilities.
